The BMI is defined the body mass divided by the square of the body height, and is expressed units of KG/M2,resulting from mass in Kilograms and height in metres.

BMI = Weight(kg)/height(m)2

  • Your BMI Value is Less than 18.5,You are Under weight.
  • Your BMI Value is Greater than 18.5 and Less than 24.9,You are Normal weight.
  • Your BMI Value is Greater than 25 and Less than 29.9,You are Over weight.
  • Your BMI Value is Greater than 30 and Less than 34.9,You are Obesity class-1.
  • Your BMI Value is Greater than 35 and Less than 39.9,You are obesity class-2
  • Your BMI Value is Greater than 40,You are obesity class-3
